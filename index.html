"We hadn't eaten in two days. Then the boat arrived."

Reena Bori, 34, and her four children spent 48 hours on the roof of their submerged home in Kamrup District. When the Hope-funded relief boat reached her, it carried 20 litres of clean water and emergency food packs — enough for her family for 5 days. "I kept telling my children help was coming," she said. "I didn't know if I was telling the truth. But it came."

Juba Region, South Sudan — July 2, 2026
🏥 Medical Response

"We were out of ORS. Then a truck arrived at midnight."

Dr. Amara Osei, running the Hope-partnered Riverside Clinic, had treated 80 cholera patients in a single day with no remaining oral rehydration salts. Hope's emergency logistics team activated a midnight supply drop. By morning, 600 ORS packets and 200 antibiotic courses had arrived. "Three of those patients would not have survived the night," Dr. Osei said quietly.

Built on Trust

You'll Always Know Where Your Money Goes

We believe transparency is the foundation of trust. Here's exactly how we keep every rupee accountable.

✅

NGO Verification

Every partner is legally registered and vetted before listing. Credentials are publicly visible on each campaign.

📍

GPS-Tracked Dispatch

Relief supplies are tracked from distribution hubs to final delivery. You receive location-stamped updates as aid moves.

📊

Audited Spending

Independent auditors review every campaign. Full spending reports published within 30 days of each operation closing.

📬

Direct Updates to You

After donating, get real photo and text updates from field coordinators — the actual humans doing the work you funded.

🔒

Secure Payments

All transactions are encrypted and processed through RBI-compliant gateways. Your data is never stored on our servers.

🌐

Public Ledger

Every donation and expense is logged on a public ledger — visible to anyone, anywhere. Nothing hidden, everything open.
